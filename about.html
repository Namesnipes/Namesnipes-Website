> about

I am a computer science major at the University of British Columbia, but I didn't start there. My journey in the field began in 2011, when I discovered a love for programming and started experimenting with small scripts and eventually moved on to creating games and websites.

While it may seem like I am exclusively a front-end developer, I have many other skills which I have acquired through a history of self-directed learning and exploration. This includes hands-on experience in game development, app development, software engineering, networking, and cyber security and more.

What I love most is learning something new and sharing it in a way that's both concise and easy for others to understand. That's why I'm particularly interested in artificial intelligence, and you can expect to see some exciting projects related to genetic algorithms on my GitHub in the future! I put a lot of effort into making sure the process is well explained in each project's readme.md, so that anyone can learn and understand the concepts behind my work.
